

## Dance Performances in Mangaung Community — Website Plan

### Design
- **Bold & vibrant** African-inspired color palette (warm oranges, deep reds, golden yellows, earthy browns)
- Large hero images/videos, energetic typography, geometric African patterns as accents
- Fully responsive, accessible design

### Pages & Structure

**1. Home Page**
- Full-width hero banner with project title "Dance Performances in Mangaung Community" and tagline
- Brief introduction to the project and Mangaung's cultural heritage
- Featured dance cards linking to individual dance pages
- Highlight reel video section (placeholder)

**2. Dances Section (3 dance pages)**
Each dance gets its own detailed section/page:
- **Ho Kgiba (Sotho Dance)** — primary featured dance
- **Pantsula** — popular township dance
- **Mohobelo** — traditional Basotho men's dance

Each includes:
- Historical Background
- Cultural Significance
- Artistic Elements (music, costumes, choreography)
- Embedded performance video (placeholder)
- Photo gallery (placeholder images)

**3. About the Project**
- Project objectives and scope
- Team members section with roles (Project Manager, Risk Manager, etc.)
- Stakeholder engagement highlights

**4. Gallery**
- Photo & video grid showcasing multimedia content
- Placeholder images with African dance themes

**5. Contact / Community**
- Encourage community interaction and feedback
- Contact form or info section

### Navigation
- Sticky top navbar with logo, Home, Dances (dropdown), About, Gallery, Contact
- Mobile hamburger menu

### Content
- All sections use placeholder text and images for now, structured so real content can be swapped in easily
